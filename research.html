Research

Research Interests

The group focuses on electronic-structure studies of materials and interfaces, with strong emphasis on both discovery and the tools needed to investigate them.

Core themes

  • Quantum materials
  • Layered heterostructures
  • Oxide quasicrystals
  • Adsorbate systems
  • Instrument development

Quantum Spin Hall Materials

Investigating electronic structure and interface behavior in topological and quantum materials.

Layered Heterostructures

Understanding mechanically stacked and engineered layered systems through surface-sensitive probes.

Metallic and Oxide Quasicrystals

Exploring complex surface order, structure, and growth pathways in quasicrystalline systems.

Organic and Inorganic Adsorbates

Studying adsorption, interface formation, and structure-property relationships on prepared surfaces.

Instrumentation

Designing and fabricating state-of-the-art tools that expand what the lab can measure and build.

Collaborative Scope

Projects span materials physics, electronics, software, and measurement design for hands-on researchers.
