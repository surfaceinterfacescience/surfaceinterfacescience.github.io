How to write to us

When contacting the lab, it helps to mention your background, skills, what kind of role you are looking for, and whether you are especially interested in materials, vacuum systems, electronics, PCB design, FPGA work, or software interfacing.

Please also note that accommodation or financial support for short visits may not always be available. If that is not a limitation for you, mention it explicitly in your message.
