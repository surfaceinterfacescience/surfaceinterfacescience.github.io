  • Building measurement systems that connect control electronics with live data workflows

National Quantum Mission Based STM Project

STM setup development aimed at exploring the local atomic structure of novel materials together with their local spectroscopic information at low temperature.

FIST Supported Cluster Deposition Chamber Project

Fund for Improvement of S&T Infrastructure (FIST) supported cluster deposition chamber design and development for department-level usage.

The plan includes one central chamber for sample exchange and about four satellite chambers supporting metal evaporation, patterning, organic deposits, and thermal operation sources. A glove box is intended in one of the organic chambers for quick sensitive in situ measurements.
