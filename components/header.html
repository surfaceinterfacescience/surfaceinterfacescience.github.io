Quick view of Laboratory

Building SIS Lab at IIT Bombay around ultrahigh-vacuum science, microscopy, spectroscopy, instrumentation, and low-budget sustainable experimental development.
  • Home-made STM setup integrating hardware, electronics, and software
  • UHV photoemission spectroscopy facility for surface-sensitive measurements
  • Development of inverse photoemission spectroscopy capability
  • Use of commercial STM setup under National Quantum Mission support
  • Development of a controlled-environment cluster deposition facility under FIST (DST)
  • Collaborative fabrication and instrument development with cost-conscious design philosophy
