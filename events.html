Periodic Lab Open Day

SIS Lab is open to organizing periodic lab open days for students and visitors who would like to explore very short-term project possibilities, internships, summer internships, MSc projects, or BTP projects.

This is a good way to visit the lab, understand the working style of the group, and discuss whether there is a suitable project direction matching your background and interests.

Open day visits are especially useful for students considering internships, MSc projects, BTP, PhD-JRF positions, or other short-term opportunities in the lab.

How to plan a visit

To reserve a slot, you may visit the lab and discuss a possible date and time with the team members. If you are travelling from another location, feel free to contact the lab in advance so that a suitable time can be arranged.

Short visits can be useful for candidates considering one-week exploratory projects, internships, summer internships, MSc projects, or BTP work.
