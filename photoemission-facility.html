Technique

Photoemission Facility

The facility supports photoemission spectroscopy with X-ray and ultraviolet sources, hemispherical electron energy analysis, and surface preparation workflows.

Equipment

Monochromatized and non-monochromatized X-ray sources, ultraviolet sources, and hemispherical energy analysis.

Surface Preparation

Ar sputtering, annealing, and scraping tools support sample preparation before measurement.

Methods

XPS, UPS, and inverse photoemission development under planning and progress.

Evaporation facility development

The lab is also developing a thermal evaporator suitable for inorganic elemental metals and organic molecules. This is a good entry point for collaborators interested in experimental build work.
